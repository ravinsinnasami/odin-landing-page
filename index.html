Indian Dishes

Idli is an excellent breakfast dish. Its soft and fluffy, suitable to be eaten plain or with curry or chutney.

Banana Leaf Rice is the go to lunch dish. Often paired with a variety of vegetables and papadam.

Samosa is one of the go to snacks for Indians. Common filling includes potato and some variation contains chicken.

Briyani is a well known rice dish that can be consumed either for lunch or dinner.Great when paired with chicken masala.

Indian Food is like a classic musical raga. It takes time to build up to a crescendo.
